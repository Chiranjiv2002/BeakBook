Features

Learn About Birds

Discover information about different bird species, including their habits, habitats, and migration patterns.

Bird Search

Search for specific bird species using keywords or browse through a list of birds to find the ones you're interested in.

Bird Recognition

Image Recognition

Identify bird species by uploading images of birds taken from your device's camera or gallery.

Audio Recognition

Listen to bird calls and songs and let the app identify the bird species based on their vocalizations.

Camera Recognition

Point your device's camera at a bird and let the app recognize the species in real-time.

Like Birds

Like your favorite birds to keep track of them and receive updates about sightings and news related to those species.
